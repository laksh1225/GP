Accounting

Accounting courses cover the fundamentals and techniques of financial management, accounting, and taxes. 
These programs seek to prepare students for careers in finance, accounting, and business management.
Combining both academic and professional paths it would take 8-9 years.
Entrance exams include CPT, IPCC, and CA final.
